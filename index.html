About Us

Who am I?

We are a team of students at KCGI who are willing to provide any kind of support to the visiting international students so that they don’t have to face any difficulty. We want them to fully concentrate on their study. We provide financial support to the needy and deserving students, academic help and counselling, educational and course-related assistance and counselling, harassment-related counselling and support, special assistance for differently-abled students, laboratory support, administrative support and many more. Similarly, we also provide assistance in registering in ward office, opening new bank account, getting new sim card and internet connection, getting ICOCA card and credit card etc. We help new international students to get affordable rooms and furniture as well as other accessories. We guide students on where to get food stuff at cheaper rates. Apart from these, we will arrange various programs so that new comers can easily interact with the previous students, keeping in mind the social distancing rules. Besides, we guide new students on how to travel via trains and buses, how to find part-time.
