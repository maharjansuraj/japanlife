Living away from home and dealing with a new academic system gives you both excitement and challenges. If you encounter difficulties, be sure to seek help from student support resources on campus early so that we have better chance of supporting you overcome challenging situation.

We offer walk-in one stop services at “Support Desk for International Students and Studying Abroad”. We also offer individual/private session to improve your situation. Further this also gives you the opportunities to connect you with on/off-campus community so that you can have a chance to deepen your understanding toward Japan and Japanese society. We also help international students with finding the career possibilities in Japan.
